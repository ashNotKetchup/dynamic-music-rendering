Thirty-Two Tones for Ensemble

Leader:

1 – Play thirty-two sequential notes, then


2 – Invite the ensemble to play a number of chords, repeat until they are all in unison


After the first step has been excecuted, the first two steps may coincide



Ensemble:

1 – Pick a note on the map


2 – On cue, play the note, then


3 – Select a new note by following a path the same colour as your current note


(In an instance where there is more than one possible path, occasionally favour the larger path)


Repeat steps two and three until you reach a note with no paths, then repeat step two


The piece concludes when all players are repeatedly in unison
